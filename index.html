WHY I

I'm a NYC-based data analyst and visual arts graduate who's spent the last year at the intersection of brand analytics and creative execution - and BOOKED & BUSY is exactly where those two worlds collide.

Your work with Allbirds, Reebok, and Kopari shows me an agency that understands how culture actually travels: not through impressions, but through moments. The Coachella brand trip, the Stanley Tucci dinner, the BetterDays pop-up - these are campaigns that respect their audience. That's the kind of work I want to be inside of.

I bring SQL, Python, Tableau, and Figma and I've used all of them. But beyond the tech stack, I think like a creative. My Visual Arts minor wasn't a hobby; it's how I approach every brief. I built a live AI-powered trend intelligence app for fashion. I know how to make things look right and how to measure whether they worked.

You said catch your attention. Consider it caught. I'm ready to start.
